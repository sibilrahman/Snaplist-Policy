Privacy Policy for Snaplist

Last Updated: April 25, 2025

Welcome to Snaplist, a list management application developed by Snaplist Team ("we," "us," or "our"). Snaplist is currently in beta, and we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Snaplist mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve the App’s functionality. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and account credentials (via email and password authentication). We may also collect device identifiers to enable synchronization across devices.
  • Snap and Message Details: We collect data related to the lists you create, including list names, list items (referred to as "snap details"), reminder settings, and any messages or notes associated with your lists (referred to as "message details").
  • Non-Personal Information: We collect usage data, such as how you interact with the App, to enhance your experience and improve the App.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device, including the operating system, device model, and IP address, to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.
  • Offline Data: Data you create while offline (e.g., lists and reminders) is stored locally on your device and synced to our servers when you reconnect to the internet.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core App functionality, such as creating, managing, and organizing lists and setting reminders.
  • Authenticate users and enable synchronization of lists across multiple devices via Firebase Authentication and Firestore.
  • Improve the App by analyzing usage patterns and identifying areas for enhancement during the beta phase.
  • Ensure the security and integrity of the App.

Note: The App is currently in beta, and we may use aggregated, anonymized data to test and refine features.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (provided by Google LLC) for authentication (Firebase Authentication) and data storage (Firebase Firestore). Your information is shared with Firebase to enable account management and synchronization. Firebase is contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or government request.
  • Business Transfers: If we merge with or are acquired by another company, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device for offline access and in Firebase Firestore for synchronization. We use Firebase’s security features, including encryption for data in transit and at rest, to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. As the App is in beta, we are actively monitoring and improving our security practices.

5. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

  • Account Information: You can update your account information (e.g., name, email) or delete your account by accessing the App’s settings or contacting us.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your lists and associated data from the App. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at sibilrahmanp@gmail.com.
  • Notifications: In the future, if the App implements push notifications, you will be able to enable or disable them in your device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at sibilrahmanp@gmail.com.

7. International Users

The App is operated from India. If you are located outside India, your information may be transferred to and processed in India or other countries where Firebase servers are located (e.g., the United States). By using the App, you consent to this transfer and processing.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves, especially during the beta phase, or to reflect changes in legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or by other means (e.g., email). The updated policy will be effective as of the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
